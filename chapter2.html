The Story

After his ocean expedition revealed millions of unknown organisms, Venter turned his genomic lens on humanity itself. In 2014 he founded Human Longevity Inc. with one audacious goal: sequence one million human genomes and use AI to decode the relationship between DNA and disease — before symptoms ever appear.

His clinic in La Jolla offered the world's most comprehensive health screening: full genome sequence, whole-body MRI, metabolomics, microbiome analysis — all integrated by machine learning. For the first time, the body's genetic blueprint could be read like a warning system.

In 2026, he launched Diploid Genomics Inc., taking this further with AI models that analyze full diploid genome sequences to predict complex disease outcomes with unprecedented precision.
